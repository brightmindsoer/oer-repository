Privacy Policy

Bright Minds ("we", "our", or "us") operates Bright Minds OER, accessible at https://brightmindsoer.github.io. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide while using our website.

Information We Collect​

  1. Contact Information: When you voluntarily provide it, we collect your email address and any additional information you provide when contacting us through the website.
  2. Usage Data: We may also collect information about how you access and use the website, such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps. This data is collected anonymously and used for analytical purposes only.

Use of Information​

  1. Communication: We use the contact information you provide to respond to your inquiries and communicate with you.
  2. Analytics: Usage data is used to analyze trends, administer the website, and gather demographic information about our user base. This helps us improve the website and tailor our content to better meet your needs.

Data Sharing​

  1. Third-Party Services: We may use third-party services, such as website hosting providers or analytics tools, to help operate our website. These service providers may have access to your information solely for the purpose of performing these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
  2. Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information in response to lawful requests by public authorities, including to meet national security or law enforcement requirements.

Data Security​

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Resource Citations​

All resources provided on our website that are subject to Creative Commons licenses are properly cited according to the terms of those licenses. We respect the intellectual property rights of content creators and attribute all resources accordingly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy​

We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us​

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at jlmati.up.edu.ph
